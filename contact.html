Fireweed

Fireweed is a perennial herbaceous plant in the willowherb family Onagraceae. It is native throughout the temperate Northern Hemisphere, including large parts of the boreal forests. Under some schemes, fireweed is classified in the genus Chamerion rather than Epilobium.

Lupine

They are referred to as 'lupin' plants and are also known as 'bluebonnets' in some areas.

Bloodroot

Bloodroot is a perennial flowering herb native to eastern North America. It has been used for inflammation, cough, infections, as an antiplaque agent, and for cancer treatment.
